Privacy Policy

VoidFocus

Effective Date: March 26, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 26, 2026

Quick Summary

  • All your data stays on your device — we have no servers
  • We do not track you, profile you, or sell your data
  • No advertising SDKs, no analytics sent to third parties
  • Screen time data is processed locally and never leaves your phone
  • Subscription payments are handled securely by Apple and Google

1. Introduction

BeCaptain Studio ("we", "us", "our") built VoidFocus ("the App") as a productivity application that helps users reduce screen time through focus sessions, app blocking, and gamification. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using VoidFocus, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

VoidFocus stores all data locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. This data is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any servers.

Data Category Examples Purpose
Focus Sessions Duration, completion status, session tag (work, study, etc.) Track your productivity and display analytics
Gamification Progress XP, level, streak count, cards earned, challenges Power the reward and motivation system
App Blocking Data List of apps you choose to block per session Enforce focus sessions by restricting selected apps
Preferences Notification settings, sound toggle, theme choice Personalize your experience
Onboarding Selections Age range, break preference, habits to quit Tailor onboarding and session recommendations
Subscription Status Pro/free status, plan type, expiry date Unlock premium features locally

2.2 Device Permissions

VoidFocus requests the following permissions to deliver its core functionality:

Permission Platform Why We Need It
Screen Time / Family Controls iOS Block selected apps during focus sessions using Apple's Screen Time API
Usage Access Android Detect foreground apps to enforce app blocking during sessions
Display Over Other Apps Android Show blocking overlay when a restricted app is opened
Notifications Both Send local reminders and streak alerts (no push servers involved)
Motion Sensors (Gyroscope) Both 3D tilt effect on collectible cards (Pro feature, real-time only, never stored)

All permissions are optional. You can deny or revoke any permission at any time through your device settings. Denying certain permissions may limit specific features (e.g., app blocking requires usage access).

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be explicit about what VoidFocus does not access:

  • Location / GPS data
  • Contacts, calendar, or photos
  • Microphone, camera, or biometric data
  • Browsing history or website visits
  • Call logs, SMS, or messaging content
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID)
  • Device unique identifiers (UDID, serial number)
  • Health or fitness data
  • Clipboard contents
  • Keystroke or input logging

3. How We Use Your Information

Since all data remains on your device, "use" means local processing only:

  • Display your analytics dashboard — session history, streaks, heatmaps, and productivity stats are computed from your local database
  • Power gamification — XP calculations, level progression, card rewards, and challenge tracking
  • Enforce focus sessions — blocking selected apps for the duration you choose
  • Schedule local notifications — daily reminders and streak-at-risk alerts
  • Export your data — generate a CSV file of your sessions when you request it

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

We use RevenueCat to manage Pro subscriptions. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes:

  • Your subscription status and entitlements
  • Product identifiers and purchase receipts
  • Trial and expiration dates

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or any personal data from VoidFocus. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We never see or store your payment details.

4.2 Apple App Store / Google Play Store

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed by the respective platform stores. Their privacy policies apply:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4.3 No Analytics, Advertising, or Tracking SDKs

VoidFocus does not integrate Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, Amplitude, Mixpanel, AdMob, or any other analytics, advertising, or tracking framework. We have zero visibility into how you use the App.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Location: All data is stored in a private SQLite database within the App's sandboxed storage on your device
  • Encryption: Your device's OS-level file protection encrypts the database at rest
  • Shared data (iOS only): Session state is shared via a secure App Group (group.com.becaptainstudio.voidfocus) between the main app and its Screen Time extensions — this data never leaves your device
  • No cloud sync: We do not back up or sync your data to any cloud service. If you uninstall the App, your data is permanently deleted

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists on your device until:

  • You uninstall VoidFocus — all data is permanently deleted by the operating system
  • You clear app data through your device settings
  • You export via CSV — you control where the exported file goes

Since we do not store any data on remote servers, there is no server-side data to delete. Uninstalling the App is a complete and permanent deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

VoidFocus is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

The App uses Apple's Family Controls framework solely for app blocking functionality, not for parental monitoring. If you believe a child has provided data through the App, please contact us so we can assist.

8. International Users

8.1 GDPR (European Economic Area)

Since VoidFocus processes all data locally on your device and does not transmit personal data to external servers, the App operates with minimal data processing. Your legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (providing the app functionality you requested). You have the right to:

  • Access your data — all data is visible within the App's analytics and settings
  • Export your data — use the CSV export feature
  • Delete your data — uninstall the App or clear app data
  • Restrict processing — revoke permissions in device settings

8.2 CCPA (California)

We do not sell, share, or disclose personal information to third parties for commercial purposes. Since all data stays on your device, there is no personal information to sell.

8.3 LGPD (Brazil)

VoidFocus armazena todos os dados localmente no seu dispositivo. Nenhum dado pessoal e transmitido para servidores externos. Voce pode exercer seus direitos de acesso, exportacao e exclusao conforme descrito acima.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • Revoke permissions at any time through your device's Settings app
  • Disable notifications within VoidFocus settings or your device settings
  • Export your data as CSV from the analytics screen
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • Manage subscriptions through the App Store or Google Play Store
