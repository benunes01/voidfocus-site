Privacy & Wellbeing

Your data stays on your device

100% Offline

VoidFocus stores all your data locally on your device. Your focus sessions, card collection, XP, streaks — everything stays on your phone. We don't collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

No account required. No cloud sync. No analytics tracking. No ads.

Screen Time & App Blocking

On iOS, VoidFocus uses Apple's official Screen Time API (Family Controls framework) to block apps during focus sessions. This is the same secure, privacy-respecting API that Apple uses for its own Screen Time features. The app requests authorization through Apple's standard consent flow, and users have full control over which apps are blocked and for how long.

On Android, VoidFocus uses the Usage Stats API and Overlay permission to monitor and block selected apps during focus sessions. These permissions are requested transparently during onboarding.

App blocking is entirely voluntary and user-controlled. Users choose which apps to block, set their own session durations, and can end any session at any time.

Purpose

VoidFocus exists to help people develop healthier relationships with their phones. The gamification system (cards, XP, levels) is designed to make building focus habits enjoyable and rewarding — not to create a new addiction. All gamification features are free and will always be free.
